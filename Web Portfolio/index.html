About Me

I'm a 19-year-old undergraduate deeply immersed in the world of cybersecurity. Currently studying this dynamic field, my primary coding language is C++, complemented by my enjoyment of working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for web development projects. I also serve as a teaching assistant, helping first-year university students grasp the essentials of C++.

Prior to my tech pursuits, I was a childcare assistant, instilling in me a sense of responsibility and empathy. During weekends back home, I marshal for the junior park-run, fostering community and well-being. My journey is marked by a dedication to cybersecurity and a commitment to making a meaningful impact in this ever-evolving landscape.
